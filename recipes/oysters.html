Oysters on the Half Shell with Rose Mignonette

Great oysters are delicious "naked," as Island Creek founder Skip Bennett says, but sometimes chef Jeremy Sewall dresses them up with a mignonette (vinegar sauce) like this one, made with sparkling ros√©.

  • 1/2 cup sparkling rose
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 2 dozen oysters, shucked
  • Shaved ice, for serving

In a bowl, combine the rose, shallots, vinegar and pepper. Arrange the oysters on a bed of ice and serve with the mignonette.
